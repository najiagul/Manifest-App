Manifest

Manifest is a web-application intended for all users wanting to schedule, prioritize and organize their day-to-day tasks in a coherent and seamless manner. With Manifest, users can accomplish what's important for them each day, set reminders and deadlines, schedule events, digitally sketch what's on their minds, and focus on what matters.

Canvas

Use our canvas feature to draw out your ideas didgitally with a variety of colors and markers.

To-do list

Plan out your day first thing in the morning using our to-do list feature to get the utmost productivity out of your day.

Sticky notes

Note down your important tasks using our sticky notes feature with the option to categorize them according to their urgency.
