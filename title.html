manifest

Manifest is a web-application intended for all users wanting to schedule, prioritize and organize their day-to-day tasks in a coherent and seamless manner. With Manifest, users can accomplish what's important for them each day, set reminders and deadlines, schedule events, digitally sketch what's on their minds, and focus on what matters.
